Welcome to St. John's island and Lazarus island

St.John's island and Lazarus island is roughly 6.5 kilometres to the south of mainland Singapore. Through the years, St John's island has been used for many purposes. In the late 1800s, it was used to house diseased immigrant. Nowadays, it is a quiet and peaceful island with pristine beaches and nature trails. Modern day Singaporeans tend to travel to these islands for a short getaway.

St John's island is connected to lazarus island via a bridge.

What to do there

There are many activities to do on St. John's island and Lazarus island. At present, St John's island is a popular getaway spot for locals and tourists alike. The rustic vibe and non-commercialized setting of the island makes it a draw for people who just wants to escape from the concrete jungle that is Singapore, for half a day! Swimming on the beaches and having a picnic with friends and family is a must on the island! You can also go on the St John's Island Trial or visit the Sisters' Island Marine Park Public Gallery there.
